## Mark Six Lottery Information

Mark Six is a popular lottery game where:
- 6 winning numbers are drawn from 1 to 49
- 1 extra number is also drawn
- Players select 6 numbers from 1 to 49
- Prizes are awarded based on how many numbers match

### Prize Divisions

- **First Prize**: 6 numbers match (Jackpot)
- **Second Prize**: 5 numbers + extra number
- **Third Prize**: 5 numbers
- **Fourth Prize**: 4 numbers + extra number
- **Fifth Prize**: 4 numbers
- **Sixth Prize**: 3 numbers + extra number
- **Seventh Prize**: 3 numbers
